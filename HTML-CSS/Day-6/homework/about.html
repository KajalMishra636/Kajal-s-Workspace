How we came to be

In 2020, a group of likeminded, passionate, and experienced individuals of the Hamilton, New Zealand disability community were each following the social media pages of the Magical Bridge Foundation in the USA. Magical Bridge foundation is a not-for-profit that advocates for, designs and builds playgrounds and parks for children and adults of all ages, abilities, sizes.

Already connected personally, they joined together to discuss this innovative and inclusive playground design and investigate the possibility of a magical playground being built in their home community, Hamilton. This conversation was the beginning of the Magical Bridge Trust, New Zealand. The dream to build a Magical Bridge Foundation Playground at Claudelands Park for all members of our community to enjoy and benefit from, is now becoming closer to a reality.
