1. Spicy Chicken Tikka Frankie


The **Spicy Chicken Tikka Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a flavor-packed delight that brings the heat and heart of Indian street food to your plate. Juicy chicken, marinated in a bold blend of tikka spices, is grilled until smoky and succulent, then nestled into a warm, soft roti. Crisp onions add a satisfying crunch, while a drizzle of zesty mint chutney cuts through the spice with a burst of freshness. It’s a handheld masterpiece—perfect for those who crave a quick, fiery bite with soul-warming flair.


2. Paneer Masala Frankie



The **Paneer Masala Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a vegetarian treasure that brings bold Indian flavors to life in every bite. Succulent paneer cubes, bathed in a luscious tomato-based masala, are spiced just right with hints of cumin, coriander, and a touch of heat, then lovingly wrapped in a warm, soft roti. Crisp, colorful veggies add a delightful crunch, while a smear of tangy chutney ties it all together, creating a handheld masterpiece that’s as comforting as it is crave-worthy—perfect for a quick meal or a satisfying snack on the go.


3. Aloo Chaat Frankie


The **Aloo Chaat Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a street-food lover’s dream, blending the zesty chaos of chaat with the convenience of a wrap. Perfectly spiced potatoes, tangy with chaat masala and a hint of cumin, are piled into a soft roti, topped with cool yogurt and a sprinkle of crispy sev for that signature crunch. A drizzle of tamarind chutney adds a sweet-sour kick, making this Frankie a vibrant, handheld explosion of flavors that’s messy in all the right ways—ideal for a quick, playful bite that satisfies both your spice cravings and your soul.


4. Mutton Keema Frankie


The **Mutton Keema Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a hearty, flavor-packed treat that brings bold, meaty goodness to the table. Minced mutton, slow-cooked with a robust mix of Indian spices like garam masala and chili, is tucked into a warm roti, its rich, savory juices soaking into every bite. Paired with sharp pickled onions for a tangy contrast, this Frankie delivers a satisfying punch of protein and spice, making it a go-to for meat lovers craving a handheld feast that’s as comforting as it is intense.


5. Egg Bhurji Frankie


The **Egg Bhurji Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a deliciously simple yet flavorful twist on a classic Indian staple. Fluffy scrambled eggs, spiced with a lively blend of turmeric, chili, and cumin, are mixed with juicy tomatoes and onions for a burst of freshness, then wrapped snugly in a warm roti. A dash of green chutney adds a fiery kick, turning this Frankie into a quick, protein-packed delight that’s perfect for breakfast on the go or a late-night snack with soul-soothing vibes.


6. Veggie Tadka Frankie

The **Veggie Tadka Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a vibrant celebration of fresh, wholesome flavors wrapped in a convenient package. A medley of colorful vegetables—think carrots, peas, and bell peppers—is stir-fried with a fragrant tadka of cumin, coriander, and a hint of mustard seeds, then folded into a soft roti. A smear of cool mint sauce adds a refreshing zing, making this Frankie a light yet satisfying option that bursts with earthy spices and veggie goodness, perfect for a guilt-free indulgence any time of day.


7. Butter Chicken Frankie

The **Butter Chicken Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a luxurious twist on a beloved classic, delivering creamy decadence in every bite. Tender chicken pieces, simmered in a velvety blend of butter, tomatoes, and fragrant spices like garam masala, are wrapped in a warm roti, letting the rich sauce soak into the folds. A sprinkle of fresh cilantro adds a bright finish, making this Frankie a handheld indulgence that’s both comforting and indulgent—perfect for those craving the iconic taste of butter chicken on the go.


8. Fish Amritsari Frankie

The **Fish Amritsari Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a crispy, spice-laden delight that brings Punjab’s bold flavors to your fingertips. Succulent fish fillets, coated in a zesty batter of chickpea flour, ajwain, and fiery red chili, are fried to golden perfection and nestled into a soft roti. A squeeze of fresh lemon and a dollop of tangy chutney elevate the crunch, making this Frankie a seafood lover’s dream—bursting with Amritsari flair and perfect for a satisfying, handheld treat with a kick.


9. Schezwan Frankie

The **Schezwan Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a fiery Indo-Chinese fusion that packs a punch for spice enthusiasts. A sizzling mix of veggies—or your choice of protein like chicken or paneer—is stir-fried in a bold Schezwan sauce, blending smoky chilies, garlic, and a hint of soy, then wrapped tightly in a warm roti. The heat hits just right, balanced by the subtle chew of the wrap, making this Frankie a thrilling handheld adventure that’s perfect for those who love their flavors loud and unapologetic.


10. Cheese Burst Frankie

The **Cheese Burst Frankie** at Uncle Frankie’s is a gooey, indulgent treat that takes comfort food to the next level. A warm roti encases a generous layer of melted cheese that oozes with every bite, paired with a mild, spiced filling of veggies or tender chicken for just the right kick. The creamy richness blends seamlessly with subtle hints of Indian spices, making this Frankie a irresistible handheld delight—perfect for cheese lovers craving a satisfying, melt-in-your-mouth experience that’s both fun and flavorful.
